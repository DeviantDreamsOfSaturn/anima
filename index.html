Our Philosophy

Every animal carries an ancient soul

Your pet arrived in your life for a reason. Through tarot, aura photography, and past life readings, we help you understand the deeper bond you share — and the wisdom your companion brings.

Anima Animal was founded on the belief that animals are not just companions but spiritual guides in their own right. Each session is a gentle, reverent inquiry into the unseen life your pet carries with them — across time, across lifetimes.
